Coffee & Health

Benefits

Coffee’s health benefits have been extensively researched and generally agreed upon.

Improve Brain Function

Extensive research shows that coffee improves various aspects of brain function, including memory, mood, vigilance, energy levels, reaction times and overall cognitive function.

Boost Metabolism

Coffee helps many lose weight, boosting metabolic rates by 3 -11%. Athletes who took in caffeine pre-exercise burned 15% more calories for three hours post-exercise than those who didn’t.

Enhance Performance

Coffee dramatically enhances performance in high intensity exercise, 11-12%, on average. Cyclists completed 15% to 23% more work during the caffeine trial than the other two trials.

Questions & Answers

What are the health benefits of coffee?

Drinking coffee can lead to numerous health benefits, including but not limited to: reducing the risk of chronic disease, extending lifespan, improved cognitive health, reduced inflammation, protecting the cardiovascular system and aiding in weight loss.

What is the difference between arabica and robusta?

Arabica beans and robusta beans are two different species of coffee. They are the primary species of coffee that find their way into the American cup. The general differences are those of taste, and the conditions under which the two species differ in production.

How Much Coffee Should You Drink?

Most studies on the health benefits of coffee focus on 3-5 cups of brewed coffee a day. However, certain health benefits increase significantly the more coffee you drink, up to 8-10 cups, although we are not recommending you drink that much coffee.
